L's Investigation Team Guide: Leading the Hunt for Kira

Last Updated: November 19, 2024

Congratulations on becoming L, the most crucial player in DEATH NOTE Killer Within. As L, you hold immense power but also carry great responsibility. Your performance can make or break the Investigation team's success.

This comprehensive guide will walk you through the intricacies of leading the investigation, protecting yourself, and ultimately bringing Kira to justice. Remember, as L, your survival is paramount - if you fall, Team Kira claims instant victory.

Core Abilities & Resources

Vindicating Evidence

Your starting command card that spawns a blue NPC. The first Investigator to reach them will be publicly revealed as trustworthy.

  • Team Kira cannot see or interact with this NPC
  • You cannot claim it yourself - use this limitation strategically
  • Watch who ignores the opportunity - they might be Team Kira
  • Be aware that Kira can cancel this by eliminating the Collaborator

Command Cards

Special abilities that help identify trustworthy team members and progress the investigation. Each card serves a unique purpose:

  • Use them consistently to maintain investigation momentum
  • Time their deployment based on team needs
  • Consider them as tools for both verification and misdirection

Essential Strategies

1. Personal Safety Protocol

  • Maintain strict distance control - never let unverified players get close
  • Avoid participating in Team Kira's command cards - they're often traps
  • Only allow verified Investigators near you, and even then, be cautious
  • Position yourself near escape routes during meetings
  • Consider every approach as potentially hostile

2. Strategic Command Card Usage

  • Plan Vindicating Evidence deployment carefully - timing is crucial
  • Move away from NPC spawn areas to avoid suspicion
  • Monitor player reactions to verification opportunities
  • Use cards to create controlled scenarios for observation
  • Track who consistently avoids verification attempts

3. Investigation Management

  • Organize Joint Investigations between verified and suspect players
  • Create situations that force Team Kira to make mistakes
  • Keep mental notes of suspicious behavior patterns
  • Build a network of trusted investigators gradually

Advanced Communication Guide

Early Game Strategies:

Document Protection Excuse

"My task is to keep documents safe, please maintain distance."

Best used in the first few minutes when tasks are still unknown

Protocol Adherence

"I'm following strict investigation protocols for everyone's safety."

Establishes professional authority without revealing role

Late Game Tactics:

Security Concern

"I've been around too many people already, maintaining distance for safety."

More believable after several rounds of interaction

Investigation Focus

"Let's focus on the evidence we have rather than personal interactions."

Redirects attention to game objectives
