Death Note Killer Within transforms the psychological warfare of the beloved anime series into an intense multiplayer experience. About Death Note Killer Within

Death Note Killer Within brings the intense psychological battles of the Death Note series to life in an innovative online multiplayer format. As a player in Death Note Killer Within, you'll be divided into two teams, each pursuing different objectives in a high-stakes game of deception and strategy.

Whether you're wielding the Death Note as Kira or pursuing justice as L, Death Note Killer Within challenges you to outthink and outmaneuver your opponents while keeping your true identity hidden. The game masterfully captures the essence of the original series, where every conversation could be a trap and every ally a potential enemy.

Death Note Killer Within Features

Strategic Social Deduction

In Death Note Killer Within, engage in intense psychological warfare as you navigate through complex social dynamics. Every game session of Death Note Killer Within presents unique challenges and opportunities for strategic thinking.

Multiple Unique Roles

Choose your path in Death Note Killer Within: become Kira, join as a Kira Follower, take on the role of L, or serve as an Investigator. Each role in Death Note Killer Within offers distinct abilities and gameplay strategies.

Cross-Platform Multiplayer

Death Note Killer Within supports seamless cross-play between PC and PlayStation platforms, allowing up to 10 players to join together in this battle of wits and deception.

How to Play Death Note Killer Within

Choose Your Side

When you enter Death Note Killer Within, you'll be assigned to either Kira's team or L's investigators. Your objective changes based on your role - eliminate L or seize the Death Note.

Master Your Role

Each role in Death Note Killer Within has unique abilities and strategies. Learn to use your powers effectively while maintaining your cover or investigating other players.

Investigate and Deceive

Use voice chat in Death Note Killer Within to coordinate with allies or mislead opponents. Participate in investigation meetings to gather information or spread disinformation.

Customize and Progress

Death Note Killer Within features extensive customization options. Unlock new avatars, accessories, and special animations to personalize your experience.
